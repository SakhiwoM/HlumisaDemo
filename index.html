About Us

Hlumisa brings together young climate and environmental students, practitioners, and activists in Eswatini around advocacy, sustainability, collective learning, and youth-led action.

Who Hlumisa is

From concept to movement

Hlumisa was first conceived in 2020 as an interactive forum event for young climate leaders between ECCo and Bushfire Festival, before the onset of the Covid-19 pandemic disrupted the original plan.

In 2021, the revision process of Eswatini's Nationally Determined Contributions reinvigorated the idea, bringing together fresh young minds, a steering committee, and a process of planning and brainstorming for youth-led climate action.

In 2022, Hlumisa was launched at the first edition of the Green Round Table, an annual event for young environmentalists in collaboration with Bushfire.
